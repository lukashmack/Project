What is my website about?

This site's objective is to take my newsletter to the next level. Substack has been outstanding by providing a uniform design with little work. However, the time is ripe to utilize my budding HTML and CSS skills to improve it. I hope to reflect my tastes and preferences through the construction of this site. The site will house my weekly sports newsletter, which uses palatable data analytics for casual fans. I write primarily about baseball, football, and soccer.

What is the objective of my website?

In addition to providing me total control over customization, my site will aid my payment processing. My ultimate goal is to transition into a freemium model where the weekly newsletter will be free, but my primers will cost around $2 a month. Substack takes a 30% cut of all payment transfers; I could use a solution like Stripe to facilitate transactions. In this case, Stripe charges 2.9% and 30 cents per transaction -- a far cry from Substack's predatory pricing.

Who is my target audience?

My target audience is people between the ages of 20-39 who are avid sports fans but want to improve their understanding of the intersection of analytics and sports. I have around 100 subscribers currently.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

The five pages will be as follows:

  • Current Edition
  • Past Editions
  • Live Q&A
  • Subscribe / Payment
  • About Me
